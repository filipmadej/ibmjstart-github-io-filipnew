jStart is a creative and innovative team within IBM's Emerging Technologies organization. Its mission is to engage customers and clients with emerging technologies to solve their real-world challenges. Over the course of the past 16 years, jStart has engaged over 300 companies ranging from Fortune 100 companies to small startups, and has championed a number of technologies into the enterprise, including: big data, text analytics, natural language processing, machine learning, IBM Watson.
